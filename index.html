About

Indore (Pithampur), Madhya Pradesh, India

I’m a supply chain professional focused on KPI-driven execution across NPI, pricing, inventory, and training analytics. I combine program management rigor (RACI/RAID, SB/PIC, FRACAS) with data tools—Power BI, SQL, Python, and Excel—to lift availability, speed decisions, and reduce waste.
